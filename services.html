Our Services

At Solar Bankability Office, we provide premium assurance services designed to give investors confidence in renewable energy projects. Our offerings combine technical depth with strategic clarity, ensuring that every project is resilient, compliant, and truly bankable.

What We Offer

  • PV Compliance Assurance: IEC, UL compliance for investor confidence.
  • PV Risk Mapping: Tailored matrices for PV modules, inverters, and BOS component risks.
  • Failure Analysis: Root cause investigations of PV module and inverter failures.
  • Lab Support: PV module reliability testing and degradation modeling.
  • Energy Yield Prediction: Independent modeling of expected PV system output, integrating degradation rates, performance ratio, and site conditions to validate investor financial models.

By integrating consultancy expertise with practical lab support, we deliver a dual‑track model that strengthens investor confidence and accelerates project success.
